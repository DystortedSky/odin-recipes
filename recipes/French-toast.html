French Toast Recipe

Description

Yummy breakfast time french toast!

Ingrediants

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 slice thick cut white bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.

  • STEP 2

    Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.

  • STEP 3

    Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.

  • STEP 4

    Transfer to hot skillet until golden brown, about 3 to 4 minutes per side
