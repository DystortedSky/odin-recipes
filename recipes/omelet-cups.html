Hash Brown and Bacon Omelet cups recipe

Description

Tastey little omelet shots for a fancy breakfast!

Ingrediants

  • Cooking spray
  • 3 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes thawed
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melter
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mexican style four-cheese blend
  • 6 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup chopper red bell pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 slices bacon, crisp cooked and chopped
  • Chopped fresh chives

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease 12 (2 1/2-inch) muffin cups with cooking spray.

  • STEP 2

    Wrap hash browns in a clean kitchen towel and squeeze to remove as much moisture as possible.

    together hash browns, melted butter, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Press about 1/4 cup

    the hash brown mixture into bottom and up the sides of each prepared muffin cup.

  • STEP 3

    Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 18 to 20 minutes.

  • STEP 4

    Meanwhile, stir together cheese, eggs, bell pepper, and crushed red pepper (if using) in a bowl.

  • STEP 5

    Remove muffin tin from the oven. Reduce oven temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

    hash browns with half of the bacon. Top with egg mixture and remaining bacon.

    until a knife inserted into centers comes out clean, 13 to 15 minutes. Garnish with chives.
